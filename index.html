Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Hirerchy Autofill browser extension (the “Extension”) handles information. The Extension helps users complete online job application forms by filling them with details from a profile the user has saved with Hirerchy. By installing and using the Extension, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information we handle

The Extension works with profile information that the user (or Hirerchy on the user’s behalf) enters into the Hirerchy client portal. This may include:

  • Identity and contact details — name, email address, phone number, and mailing address.
  • Work-eligibility information — work authorization status, visa/sponsorship details, and related answers.
  • Professional information — work history, education, skills, and links (e.g., LinkedIn, portfolio).
  • Documents — résumé and cover letter files you choose to upload.
  • Voluntary demographic information — such as gender, race/ethnicity, veteran status, and disability status, used only to complete equal-opportunity questions where an application asks for them. Providing this information is optional.

The Extension does not collect browsing history, and it does not read or transmit the contents of pages you visit beyond the form fields it fills when you ask it to.

2. How we use the information

The information is used for a single purpose: to automatically fill job application forms on your behalf when you click “Autofill.” Specifically, the Extension:

  • Retrieves your saved profile from Hirerchy’s secure backend after you sign in.
  • Enters the relevant values into matching fields on the job application page you are viewing.

We do not use your information for advertising, and we do not sell it.

3. How the information is stored

Profile information is stored in Hirerchy’s backend, hosted on Supabase, and is protected by authentication so that only the signed-in user can access their own profile. The Extension keeps a sign-in session token in your browser’s local storage so you stay logged in; it is removed when you sign out.

4. Information sharing and disclosure

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • Information is transmitted only (a) between the Extension and Hirerchy’s backend to load your profile, and (b) into the job application website you choose to fill, at your direction, when you trigger autofill.
  • When you submit an application, the information you placed in that form is handled by that employer or job-platform under their own privacy policy, which is outside Hirerchy’s control.
  • We may disclose information if required by law.

5. Data retention and deletion

Your profile is retained for as long as your Hirerchy account is active. You can review, update, or remove your information at any time through the Hirerchy client portal, or by contacting us at the address below to request deletion of your data.

6. Security

We use industry-standard measures, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and authenticated access, to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to protect your data against unauthorized access.

7. Children’s privacy

The Extension is intended for adult job seekers and is not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.
