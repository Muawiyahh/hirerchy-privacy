Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Hirerchy and the Hirerchy Autofill browser extension (the “Extension”) handle personal information. Hirerchy provides a service in which authorized team members help complete online job applications on behalf of Hirerchy’s clients. The Extension is the tool those team members use to fill application forms with a client’s saved profile information.

This policy covers two groups: clients, whose personal information is processed so that applications can be completed for them; and authorized Hirerchy team members, who sign in to the Extension to complete those applications on a client’s behalf.

1. Information we handle

The Extension works with a client’s profile information, which the client provides through the Hirerchy client portal (or which Hirerchy enters on the client’s behalf). This may include:

  • Identity and contact details — name, email address, phone number, and mailing address.
  • Work-eligibility information — work authorization status, visa/sponsorship details, and related answers.
  • Professional information — work history, education, skills, and links (e.g., LinkedIn, portfolio).
  • Documents — résumé and cover letter files you choose to upload.
  • Voluntary demographic information — such as gender, race/ethnicity, veteran status, and disability status, used only to complete equal-opportunity questions where an application asks for them. Providing this information is optional.

The Extension does not collect browsing history, and it does not read or transmit the contents of pages beyond the form fields it fills when a team member triggers it.

2. How we use the information

The information is used for a single purpose: to complete job application forms on a client’s behalf. When an authorized team member opens a job application and clicks “Autofill,” the Extension:

  • Retrieves the selected client’s saved profile from Hirerchy’s secure backend.
  • Enters the relevant values into matching fields on the job application page being viewed.

We do not use this information for advertising, and we do not sell it.

3. Who can access the information

Profile information is stored in Hirerchy’s backend, hosted on Supabase, and is protected by authenticated access. It can be accessed by:

  • The client, who can view and edit their own profile through the Hirerchy client portal.
  • Authorized Hirerchy team members, who sign in to the Extension and access the profiles of the clients they are assigned to, in order to complete applications on those clients’ behalf.

The Extension keeps a sign-in session token in the team member’s browser local storage so they stay logged in; it is removed on sign-out.

4. Information sharing and disclosure

  • We do not sell personal information.
  • Information is transmitted only (a) between the Extension and Hirerchy’s backend to load a client’s profile, and (b) into the job application website being completed, when a team member triggers autofill.
  • Once an application is submitted, the information placed in that form is handled by the relevant employer or job platform under their own privacy policy, which is outside Hirerchy’s control.
  • We may disclose information if required by law.

5. Data retention and deletion

A client’s profile is retained for as long as their Hirerchy account is active. Clients can review, update, or remove their information at any time through the Hirerchy client portal, or by contacting us at the address below to request deletion of their data.

6. Security

We use industry-standard measures, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and authenticated access, to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to protect your data against unauthorized access.

7. Children’s privacy

The Extension is intended for adult job seekers and is not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.
